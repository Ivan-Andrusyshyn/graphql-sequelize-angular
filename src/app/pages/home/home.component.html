About Me

👋 Welcome to my website! I am dedicated to providing the best services for my clients.

This is a full-stack application that I built to help you efficiently plan and manage your daily tasks. Utilizing modern technologies such as Sequelize for database management and GraphQL for seamless API interaction, the app provides a powerful solution for task organization. 🗓️📋 My primary goal with this platform is to make task planning simple, intuitive, and highly productive, ensuring you stay on track with all your responsibilities. 🚀

My mission is to deliver top-notch quality with a customer-first approach, ensuring that productivity and ease of use go hand in hand. 🎯

Technology Stack: 🛠️ Node.js, Express, Sequelize (PostgreSQL), GraphQL, Angular for the frontend, and Material UI for a modern and responsive user interface.

From personal task management to team collaboration, this application is designed to adapt to your needs. Let’s make productivity fun and effortless! 🎉💼
